Godwin Kachi

Godwin was born in the early 80's in the ancient town of Enugu. Growing up was quite interesting for him as he loved most of the things he had to engage himself with even in those early years.

Over the years, Godwin has gained experience working in the capacity of managing multiple businesses within the professional and non-professional space, which has accorded him a handful of experience across Electricals, Automobile, Education, Agric and Health Industries.

He is a firm believer of the school of thought that prioritizes skills and integrity before monetary and immediate gratification, hence, his zeal to invest into people the various skills that will help them not only to grow but also enable them to cascade in the various communities they may find themselves.
